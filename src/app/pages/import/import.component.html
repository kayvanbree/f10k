Import songs

 Save the songs you saved on Spotify here on f10k. This is way quicker than importing your playlists, but you'll have to add songs manually to f10k if you hit the 10k limit before coming here.

Import playlists

 Traverse your playlists from Spotify and save all songs to f10k. This may take a while, as we have to wait in between playlists to prevent making too many requests to the Spotify Web API.
